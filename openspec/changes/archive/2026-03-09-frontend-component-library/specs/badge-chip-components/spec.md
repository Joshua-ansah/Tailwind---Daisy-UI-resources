## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Badges and chips are all represented
The `components/badges-chips.html` page SHALL show all DaisyUI badge variants and tag/chip patterns.

#### Scenario: Badge colours and sizes are shown
- **WHEN** the page renders
- **THEN** it SHALL show badges in all DaisyUI colours and in xs, sm, md, lg sizes

#### Scenario: Outline and ghost badge variants are shown
- **WHEN** the page renders
- **THEN** it SHALL show outline and ghost badge styles

#### Scenario: Dismissible chips and status dots are shown
- **WHEN** the page renders
- **THEN** it SHALL show pills/chips with an × dismiss indicator and coloured status dot indicators
