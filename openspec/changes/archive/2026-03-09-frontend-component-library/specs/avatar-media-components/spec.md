## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Avatar and media patterns are shown
The `components/avatars-media.html` page SHALL show avatar, image, and media placeholder patterns.

#### Scenario: Avatar sizes and shapes are shown
- **WHEN** the page renders
- **THEN** it SHALL show avatars in multiple sizes, circular and rounded-square shapes, and a placeholder initial avatar

#### Scenario: Avatar group and status indicator are shown
- **WHEN** the page renders
- **THEN** it SHALL show a stacked avatar group and an avatar with an online/offline status dot

#### Scenario: Image with caption and aspect-ratio placeholders are shown
- **WHEN** the page renders
- **THEN** it SHALL show a figure with caption and placeholder boxes in common aspect ratios (16:9, 4:3, 1:1)
